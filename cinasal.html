Chicken Inasal

Ingredients
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon calamansi juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cooking oil
Instructions
  1. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, calamansi juice, minced garlic, brown sugar,
    paprika, salt and pepper then mix.
  2. Add the chicken thighs into the marinade then let it sit for 1 hour.
  3. Heat up the grill or grill pan over medium heat then cook the chicken
    for 5-7 minutes on each side, baste your chicken with some of the marinade as you go.
  4. Remove the chicken from the grill then let it rest for a few minutes.
  5. Plate it and enjoy!
