Kwek-Kwek

Ingredients
  • 5-10 quail eggs (boiled)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Orange food coloring
  • Vegatable oil
Instructions
  1. Mix all dry ingredients add water and food coloring
    then stir until batter is smooth.
  2. Heat vegatable oil over medium-high heat and dip each quail egg in batter.
  3. Then fry until the coated eggs in batches until golden brown (3-5 mins).
  4. Remove eggs and excess oil then enjoy!
