Mango Graham

Ingredients
  • 3 large ripe mangoes
  • 200g graham (depending on size of container)
  • 300g condensed milk
  • 250ml all purpose cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions
  1. Combine all-purpose cream, condensed milk, and vanilla extract then mix.
  2. Put graham crackers on the bottom then layer with mixture and mango slices on top of mixture.
  3. Repeat layers until satisfied.
  4. Then refrigerate overnight.
  5. Plate it then enjoy!
