Sisig

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 lbs. Pork belly
  • 3 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 1 lb. pig face
  • 4 ounces chicken liver
  • 2 pieces onion
  • 1/2cup chicharon crumbled
  • 3 tablespoons Mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 quarts water
Instructions
  1. In medium-high heat boil pork belly and pig face for one hour then drain.
  2. Grill boiled meat for 5 minutes each side then chop into small pieces.
  3. Heat oil then saute onions, add chicken liver saute until cooked mash liver into small pieces.
  4. add minced meat and chicharon into the pan and stir, then add onion powder and black pepper.
  5. Pour liquid seasoning and mayonnaise stir and cook for 3 minutes.
  6. Heat a metal plate and add butter then transfer the sisig onto hot plate and enjoy!
