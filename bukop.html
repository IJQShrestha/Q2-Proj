Buko Pandan

Ingredients
  • 20 ounces young coconut strips
  • 250 ml all purpose cream
  • 150 ml condensed milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 3 ounces powdered gelatin
  • 1/2 lb pandan leaves
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon buko pandan flavoring
Instructions
  1. Boil water then add pandan leaves, cover and boil for 15 minutes.
  2. Add sugar, powdered gelatin, and buko pandan flavoring. (Stir until powder is dissolved)
  3. Then transfer mixture into a mold and let it cool.
  4. Combine young coconut strips, all purpose cream, and condensed milk.
  5. Keep chilled overnight then slice the gelatin and combine with mixture and enjoy!
