Taho

Ingredients
  • 16 oz silken tofu
  • 1 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup tapioca or sago pearls
Instructions
  1. Cook tapioca pearls according to box instructions.
  2. Combine brown sugar, water, and vanilla in a small pan
    and place over medium-high heat simmer for 2-4 minutes.
  3. Microwave the tofu for about 2-5 minutes.
  4. Then scoop the tofu into a small cup, add the brown sugar and sago and enjoy!
