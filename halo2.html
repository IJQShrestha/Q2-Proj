Halo Halo

Ingredients
  • 2 cups crushed ice
  • 2 tbsp Sweetened red beans
  • 2 slices Saba banana
  • 1 scoop Ube ice cream
  • 1 tbsp Leche flan
  • 3 tbsp Evaporated milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp corn
Instructions
  1. In a tall glass or bowl add sweetend red beans, saba, sugar, and corn.
  2. Then add crushed ice and evaporated milk then add leche flan and ube ice cream on the top.
  3. Finally mix and enjoy!
